¶ 04 The process

Four steps. Three to six weeks.

No mystery, no surprises. Here's exactly how a PixelForge project moves from first call to live site.

Discover

30-minute call to understand your business, your customers, and what success looks like. We send a written quote within 48 hours.

Design

We build a clickable design of your homepage and one inner page. You give feedback. We iterate until you say "yes, that's it."

Build

Hand-coded, page by page. We show you progress builds twice a week — no waiting until the end to see it.

Launch

We deploy to your domain, walk you through everything, and stay on call for 30 days of free tweaks and support.
